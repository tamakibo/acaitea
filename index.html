About Us

Our journey began with a deep appreciation for the unique flavor and aroma of Vietnamese tea. As we learned more about the cultivation process, we became fascinated by the farmers who work tirelessly to produce this high-quality tea. We realized that their stories deserved to be told and their tea deserved to be tasted.

How to enjoy

Each cup of tea tells a story of the land, the people, and the culture that produced it.

Step 1:

Boil the water to around 90-95°C (194-203°F)

Step 2:

Pour over the tea leaves & let it steep for about 2-3 minutes

Step 3:

Enjoy with snack
