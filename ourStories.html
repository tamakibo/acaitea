Our stories

Our mission is to highlight the amazing work done by these farmers and to share their delicious tea with the world.

Our journey began with a deep appreciation for the unique flavor and aroma of Vietnamese tea. As we learned more about the cultivation process, we became fascinated by the farmers who work tirelessly to produce this high-quality tea. We realized that their stories deserved to be told and their tea deserved to be tasted.

Our team is dedicated to building relationships with these farmers and supporting their businesses. We work directly with them to source the best tea leaves and ensure fair compensation for their hard work. By doing so, we hope to promote sustainable farming practices and empower local communities.
